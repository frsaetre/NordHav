HRP-012 Time Registration Support multiple time registration methods: physical time clocks (at processing plant), mobile app (field/sea-site workers), web interface (office staff), and manager entry on behalf of employees. Mandatory
HRP-013 Multiple Shift Patterns Handle complex shift patterns: 2-shift processing (06–14, 14–22), 3-shift smolt facility (24/7), offshore-style rotation for sea-site workers (e.g., 2 weeks on / 2 weeks off or similar), and regular office hours with flextime. Mandatory
HRP-014 Overtime Calculation Automatic overtime calculation per collective agreements and Norwegian Arbeidsmiljøloven: daily overtime thresholds, weekly overtime, weekend premiums, and maximum overtime limits (200/230 hours/year). Mandatory
HRP-015 Shift Premiums Calculate shift differentials and premiums per collective agreements: evening premium, night premium, Saturday premium, Sunday/holiday premium, and offshore/remote allowance. Mandatory
HRP-016 Time Approval Workflow Time approval by line manager before payroll processing. Highlight exceptions (overtime, absences, deviations from schedule) for approval. Mandatory
HRP-017 Working Time Compliance Monitor compliance with Norwegian working time regulations: maximum daily/weekly hours, minimum rest between shifts (11 hours), maximum overtime. Alert on violations. High
HRP-018 Project Time Registration Employees should optionally be able to register time against projects/cost activities for: maintenance projects, capital projects, and special farming operations. High

HRP-019 Annual Leave (Ferie) Manage annual leave entitlements per Norwegian Ferieloven: standard 25 days, extra week for 60+, carried-over days, holiday pay calculation (10.2% / 12.0% for 60+). Mandatory
HRP-020 Sick Leave (Sykefravær) Handle sick leave management: self-reported sick leave (egenmelding, up to 3/8 days), medical sick note (sykmelding) registration, employer period (16 days) and NAV period tracking, partial sick leave (gradert sykmelding), and return-to-work follow-up. Mandatory
HRP-021 Digital Sick Note Integration Integration with NAV's digital sick note system (digital sykmelding) for automated receipt and processing of medical sick certificates. High
HRP-022 Parental Leave Support Norwegian parental leave: calculation of parental benefit basis, mother/father quota, shared period, 80%/100% coverage options, and integration with NAV for benefit claims. High
HRP-023 Other Leave Types Support additional leave types: compassionate leave, military service, study leave, union representative leave, care leave (omsorgspermisjon), with configurable rules for each. Mandatory
HRP-024 Leave Calendar Visual leave calendar showing team leave plans, enabling managers to check coverage and avoid conflicts. High

HRP-025 Norwegian Payroll Calculation Full Norwegian payroll calculation engine: gross-to-net computation including income tax (based on tax cards from Skatteetaten), employee pension contributions, union dues, and other deductions. Mandatory
HRP-026 Collective Agreement Rules Support payroll calculations per multiple collective agreements simultaneously (NNN for processing, Fellesforbundet for operations, etc.): base rates, seniority increments, shift premiums, overtime rates. Mandatory
HRP-027 Tax Card Integration Interface with Skatteetaten for electronic tax card retrieval (elektronisk skattekort) at the start of each year and upon changes. Mandatory
HRP-028 Employer Social Contributions Calculate arbeidsgiveravgift (employer's national insurance contributions) at the correct zone-differentiated rate for each employee/location. NordHav operates across multiple employer tax zones. Mandatory
HRP-029 Pension (OTP) Calculate and manage mandatory occupational pension (obligatorisk tjenestepensjon — OTP) contributions per the OTP Act. Support both defined benefit and defined contribution schemes as applicable. Mandatory
HRP-030 Holiday Pay (Feriepenger) Calculate holiday pay at 10.2% (12.0% for 60+) of the previous year's earnings. Support June payout and handle different contractual arrangements. Mandatory
HRP-031 A-melding Reporting Generate and submit A-melding (combined monthly report of income, employment, and tax deduction information) to Skatteetaten, NAV, and SSB in the required electronic format. Mandatory
HRP-032 Payslip Distribution Generate and distribute digital payslips to employees (via employee self-service portal, email, or digital mailbox — e.g., Digipost/Altinn). Mandatory
HRP-033 Bank Payment File Generate salary payment files for bank: standard Norwegian bank file format for bulk salary payments. Mandatory
HRP-034 Year-End Reporting Support annual payroll reporting: annual summary per employee (årsoppgave), reconciliation of A-melding submissions, and reporting to Skatteetaten. Mandatory
HRP-035 Retroactive Pay Adjustments Support retroactive pay adjustments (e.g., collective agreement back-pay) with recalculation of tax and contributions. High
HRP-036 Expense Reimbursement Process employee expense claims: submission (receipt upload), approval workflow, tax treatment (per Skatteetaten rules for travel, subsistence, mileage), and payment. High
