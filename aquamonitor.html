D365 Read-Through

9 Virtual Entities

Virtual Entities expose live D365 F&O data in AquaMonitor MDA without copying data to Dataverse. Read-only, always current.

InventSiteEntity
D365 sites (mapped from nha_site registration). Used to display warehouse/site ID in MDA pen forms.
InventBatchEntity
Catch-weight batch records. Maps to nha_generation. Shows current batch inventory in sea site screens.
ProdProductionOrderEntity
Harvest production orders. AquaMonitor harvest BPF reads order status to gate harvest approval flow.
InventTransferOrderEntity
Inter-site fish transfers. Freshwater screen triggers D365 Transfer Orders; status visible in MDA.
PurchPurchaseOrderEntity
Feed and smolt purchase orders. Feed Management screen shows PO status and confirmed delivery dates.
EntAssetObjectEntity
Physical assets (pens, vessels, barges). Treatment screen links to asset for maintenance scheduling.
EntAssetWorkOrderEntity
D365 Maintenance Work Orders. Withdrawal tracker reads WO status to confirm treatment completion.
PdsMRCItemCertificationEntity
Regulatory item certifications (fish meal, additives). Displayed on feed product record for audit.
InventOnHandEntity
Live inventory on hand by site/warehouse. Displayed as available biomass in harvest planning screen.

Business Process Flow

Harvest Readiness BPF — 7 Stages

Every harvest must complete all 7 stages sequentially. Stage 3 performs an automated withdrawal-period check against nha_withdrawal. Stage 6 triggers the D365 Production Order release.

1
Harvest Planning
Weight, date, buyer, pen selection
2
Biology Sign-off
Mean weight, FCR review, population
3
Regulatory Check
Auto-check: withdrawal, lice, Mattilsynet
4
Logistics
Vessel, slaughterhouse, transport docs
5
Certification
Live weight certification & cert upload
6
D365 Release
Auto-release Production Order via AQUA-FLOW-007
7
Site Close-out
Final inventory zero-out, generation close
