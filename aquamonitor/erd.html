Core Infrastructure — 5 tables

Sites, pens, fish generations, production licenses, freshwater tanks. Every other table carries at least one FK into this domain. Note: nha_sitecode = D365 InventSiteId, nha_pencode = D365 Warehouse ID (storage dimension), nha_generationcode = D365 inventBatchId.
