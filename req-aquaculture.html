AQU-001 Long-Term Production Plan Support long-term (3–5 year) production planning incorporating: smolt release schedules, growth projections, harvest forecasts, site rotation/fallowing, and MAB (Maximum Allowed Biomass) capacity constraints. Mandatory
AQU-002 Site Register Maintain a comprehensive register of all production sites (freshwater and marine) with: site name, location (GPS coordinates), license number, MAB limit, zone affiliation, regulatory permits, site status (active/fallow/decommissioned). Mandatory
AQU-003 License Management Track production licenses (konsesjoner) with: license number, holder, MAB allocation, associated sites, regulatory conditions, and expiry/renewal dates. Mandatory
AQU-004 MAB Monitoring Real-time monitoring of standing biomass against Maximum Allowed Biomass (MAB) limits per license and total. Alerts when approaching threshold (e.g., 90%, 95% of MAB). Mandatory
AQU-005 Fallow Planning Plan and track site fallowing schedules per zone coordination agreements. Ensure minimum fallow periods are met before restocking. High
AQU-006 Pen Register Maintain a register of individual net pens at each site with: pen number, dimensions, net specification, mooring reference, current fish population, and status. Mandatory
AQU-007 Generation/Batch Tracking Track fish populations as batches or generations from egg receipt through to harvest. A generation/batch is defined by year class, origin (smolt facility), and genetic strain. Mandatory
AQU-008 Pen-Level Data Support recording and querying of all operational data at the individual pen level (population, biomass, feed, mortality, lice, treatments, water parameters). Mandatory
AQU-009 Site-Level Dashboards Provide real-time site-level dashboards showing: total biomass, feed consumption, mortality, lice levels, water temperature, and operational status for all pens. High
AQU-010 Multi-Site Overview Provide a company-wide overview screen showing all sites and their key status indicators (traffic light system for biomass, lice, health, equipment). High

AQU-015 Degree-Day Calculator Automatically calculate accumulated degree-days (temperature × days) for each batch, used to predict developmental milestones (hatching, yolk sac absorption, smoltification). High

AQU-018 Smoltification Testing Record smoltification readiness test results (ATPase, chloride cell analysis) per batch with pass/fail thresholds. High
AQU-019 Photoperiod Management Log and plan photoperiod (light regime) programs per tank or facility section. Track light hours, UV index, and regime changes. Desirable
AQU-020 Smolt Cost Accumulation Accumulate all costs to produce each smolt batch: egg cost, feed, labor, energy, vaccines, consumables, depreciation, and facility overhead. Calculate cost per smolt. Mandatory
AQU-021 Transfer to Sea Planning Plan and schedule the transfer of smolt/post-smolt to sea sites, integrating with: well-boat bookings, sea site readiness, weather windows, and health certificates. High
AQU-022 Sea Transfer Documentation Generate transfer documentation: number of fish, average weight, origin facility/tank, destination site/pen, well-boat details, health certificate, transfer date/time. Mandatory
AQU-023 Post-Smolt Module Support post-smolt grow-out phase (land-based, 100–500g) as a separate production stage with its own data capture, feeding, and cost tracking. High

AQU-024 Stocking Registration Register stocking events: date, site, pen(s), fish count, average weight at stocking, origin (smolt batch/facility), and initial biomass calculation. Mandatory
AQU-025 Daily Mortality Recording Record daily mortality per pen: number of dead fish, estimated cause of death (handling, disease, predation, environmental, unknown), and weight estimate. Mandatory
AQU-026 Mortality Analysis Analyze mortality trends over time by: pen, site, region, generation, cause of death. Provide alerts when mortality exceeds configurable thresholds. High
AQU-027 Biomass Estimation Maintain daily biomass estimates per pen using: last known fish count, cumulative mortality, estimated growth (TGC/SGR models), and periodic calibration from sampling/weighing. Mandatory

AQU-031 Feed Conversion Calculation Calculate biological FCR and economic FCR per pen, site, and generation. Display FCR trends and enable comparison against targets and benchmarks. Mandatory
AQU-032 Feed Budget vs Actual Compare actual feed consumption against feed budget (planned feeding tables) per pen and site. Highlight over/under-feeding deviations. High
AQU-033 Environmental Data Capture Capture and store environmental sensor data: water temperature, salinity, dissolved oxygen, current speed, wave height. Interface with on-site environmental monitoring systems. High
AQU-034 Algal/Jellyfish Alerts Support registration of environmental hazard events (algal blooms, jellyfish, predator sightings) with: date, severity, affected site/pens, and actions taken. High
AQU-035 Standing Biomass Report Real-time report of total standing biomass by: pen, site, license, region, and company total. With comparison to MAB limits. Mandatory
AQU-036 Pen Splitting & Combining Handle operational events where fish from one pen are split into multiple pens (e.g., during grading at sea) or combined. Maintain accurate count and biomass after such events. High
AQU-037 Inter-Site Transfer Record fish transfers between sea sites: origin pen/site, destination pen/site, fish count, biomass, transfer method (well-boat), date, and reason. Mandatory

AQU-055 Gill Health Monitoring Track gill health assessments (gill scoring, histopathology results) as a distinct health parameter — critical for early detection of AGD (Amoebic Gill Disease) and other gill conditions. Desirable
