PRC-006 Production Scheduling Schedule daily and weekly production considering: raw material availability (harvest plan), customer orders, processing line capacity, staff availability, and product mix. Mandatory
PRC-007 Stunning & Slaughter Recording Record slaughter events: date, time, batch, stunning method, stunning parameters (voltage, duration), welfare compliance check, and number of fish processed. Mandatory

PRC-012 Catch-Weight / Variable Weight Full support for catch-weight (variable weight) products: individual item weight capture, case aggregation, and weight-based pricing. Critical for fresh fish where each unit has a unique weight. Mandatory
PRC-013 Filleting Line Management Track filleting operations: input volume (HOG), output by product (skin-on fillet, skin-off fillet, trimmed fillet), trimming waste, yield per line, and fillet quality metrics. High
PRC-014 Pin Bone Tracking Record pin bone removal status and quality assurance checks (e.g., X-ray or manual inspection pass/fail rates). High
PRC-015 By-Product Stream Tracking Track all by-product streams from primary processing: heads, frames, belly trim, skins, viscera, blood water. Record volumes (kg) by type per batch for costing and by-product sales. Mandatory
PRC-016 Ensilage Management Track ensilage production from processing waste: input volumes, acid addition, storage tank levels, and dispatch to by-product purchasers. Desirable
PRC-017 Ice Consumption Tracking Track ice production and consumption for packing. Monitor ice plant capacity and ice quality. Desirable

PRC-031 VAP Recipe Management Maintain production recipes/BOM (Bill of Materials) for all VAP products: ingredients (salmon, salt, sugar, spices, smoke, etc.), quantities, processing instructions, and allergen flags. Mandatory
PRC-032 Recipe Version Control Version control for recipes: track recipe changes, effective dates, reason for change, and regulatory approval status. Ensure production uses the currently approved version. Mandatory
PRC-033 VAP Production Orders Create VAP production orders: product, quantity, recipe version, batch size, scheduled production date, raw material requirements, packaging requirements. Mandatory
PRC-034 Raw Material Allocation Allocate incoming fresh fillets from primary processing to VAP production orders based on: quantity needed, quality grade, size specification, and production priority. High
PRC-035 Ingredient Lot Tracking Track ingredient lots used in each VAP production batch: salmon batch(es), salt lot, spice lot, smoke wood lot, etc. Full traceability of all inputs to each finished product batch. Mandatory
PRC-036 Smoking Process Recording Record smoking process parameters per batch: kiln number, product loaded (kg), smoking curve (temperature/time profile), wood type, start/end times, core temperature, yield. Mandatory
PRC-037 Curing / Marination Recording Record curing and marination process parameters: recipe, brine/cure composition, injection rate (if applicable), curing time, temperature, and batch volumes. Mandatory
PRC-038 Slicing Line Data Record slicing operations: input product/batch, output volume, slice thickness, pack weight target, actual pack weights, slicing yield, and waste. High
PRC-039 Packaging Machine Integration Interface with thermoforming/MAP packaging machines to capture: pack count, gas mix (CO₂%, N₂%), seal parameters, and reject counts. High
PRC-040 Metal Detection / X-Ray Record metal detection and X-ray inspection results per production run: number of packs inspected, rejects (count, reason), calibration check results. CCP (Critical Control Point) data. Mandatory
PRC-041 Checkweigh Integration Interface with checkweighers to capture: target weight, actual weights (per pack), reject count (underweight/overweight), and statistical compliance data. Mandatory
PRC-042 VAP Yield Analysis Track and report yield at each VAP process stage: raw material input → salted/cured → smoked → sliced → packed. Compare actual yield vs. recipe standard yield. High
PRC-043 Co-Product & Waste Tracking Track co-products (e.g., smoked trimmings from slicing) and waste generated during VAP processing. Allocate costs and revenue appropriately. High
PRC-044 VAP SKU Management Manage the full VAP product catalog (~120 SKUs) with: product code, description, recipe link, packaging specification, shelf life, storage conditions, nutritional information, allergen declarations, and customer-specific variants. Mandatory
PRC-045 Shelf-Life Management Assign and manage shelf life for all products based on: product type, packaging method, and storage conditions. Automatically calculate best-before dates from production date. Mandatory
