Salmon Value Chain
🏢

Corporate HQ — Bergen

NordHav Aquaculture AS headquarters at Bontelabo 2. Houses management, finance, IT, HR, quality, and central sales (85 FTE).

🐣

Smolt & Post-Smolt Facilities

RAS hatcheries in Masfjorden (12M smolt), Kvam in Hardanger (8M), and Halsa (5M). Total capacity: 25 million smolt annually.

🌊

Sea Farming Regions

28 production licenses across 4 regions: Hordaland, Sogn og Fjordane, Møre og Romsdal, and Nordland. 22 active sea sites, 42,000t MAB.

🏭

Processing — Austevoll

12,000 m² integrated complex: primary processing (180t/day), VAP facility (60t/day), cold storage. 260 FTE, peak 340. 52,000t GWE harvest annually.

💼

Commercial & Sales

Domestic offices in Bergen and Oslo. International subsidiaries: NHU (New York & Los Angeles, USA) and NHN (Amsterdam, Netherlands). ~350 customer accounts across fresh, frozen, and VAP categories.

🌍

Export Markets

NOK 4.2B revenue. EU 50% (via NHN Amsterdam), North America 12% (via NHU NY/LA), Nordics 14%, Asia-Pacific 16%, Other 8%. Products reach 100+ countries.
